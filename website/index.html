Fixed-To-Mobile Number Mapper

Map your mobile number to your fixed number.

  1. Send an SMS with the text "link 0212345678" to 0902 028 031
    • Replace 0212345678 with your fixed number
    • You will receive a 4-digit PIN
  2. Call from your fixed number to 02 3345 6835 and enter your PIN
  3. If the combinations are correct your numbers are mapped, otherwise try again
